Coming Soon in October!

Before he dies, her father gives her the king’s powerful circlet. He never told her how he ended up with it, or how the kingdom fell into rebellion.

High-spirited Roseira journeys to the king’s last stronghold, a mystical castle. But she inadvertently opens the way for an ominous knight, Livarro, to attack with his massive army.

To restore her father’s honor, she must trust a vagabond wizard named Joshua and his quirky fireball Lumen. They find a way to defend the castle with the help of Sir Michael, dashing Jeffery, and sweet but complacent Isabel.

Yet the manipulative enemy sows doubts and reveals secrets. Roseira learns the truth about Joshua’s parents, and the truth about her own. She learns just who the real enemy is.

Castle of the Aerelim is a screenplay built upon a book by St. Teresa of Avila: The Interior Castle. Includes a 1-2-3 Catholic presentation on the 7 Mansions.

Why a Screenplay?

A screenplay is like the secret code for a movie. Not many screenplays are made available to the public before production. Give yourself and your kids a well-rounded knowledge of literature in its different forms. Similar to a play, a screenplay is concise and visual, and reading one can feel almost like watching a movie! Being a screenwriter is a career. If that is what your child is called to do, give him a chance to know his calling exists!
